About Us







ACM-W'sJunior Mentorship Programme (ACM-W JuMP) aims to

build a cohesive community of undergraduate developers.

It pairs students as mentees with third and fourth year

mentors with a goal to learn technical and soft

skills and develop real-time mobile and web apps.

The programme runs from September to January.

Over the course of five months, the mentees learnt

the basics of development, plan the project and finally

built an application with the help of mentors.

This website “Serene” is built by the web development

mentees with the help of their mentors.

It is a website which focus on helping students

dealing with mental health issues in the university or outside.

The different features of this website include

blogs games and a slack channel

which aims to connect the students with their peers.
